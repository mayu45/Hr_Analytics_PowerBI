# Project Title
HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI  


# Project Description
The HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of employee attrition and workforce demographics. Designed using Microsoft Power BI, this dashboard helps the HR department identify key patterns and insights related to employee turnover, age distribution, education, salary, and job roles — enabling data-driven HR decisions and strategies.
	

# Objectives
•To analyze employee attrition trends across different demographics. <br />
•To identify key factors contributing to employee turnover. <br />
•To provide HR with actionable insights to reduce attrition and improve retention. <br />
•To visualize workforce distribution by age, education, and salary. <br />


#  Key Metrics & KPIs
•	Overall Employees: 1,470 <br />
•	Total Attrition: 237 <br />
•	Attrition Rate: 16.1% <br />
•	Average Age: 37 years <br />
•	Average Salary: 6.5K <br />
•	Years at Company: Variable across employees <br />


# Dashboard Insights
## Attrition by Education: 
Highest among Life Sciences professionals (38%). <br />

## Attrition by Age:  
Most employees leaving are between 26–35 years old. <br />

## Attrition by Salary Slab: 
Majority of attrition in the below 5K salary range. <br />

## Attrition by Job Role: 
Highest attrition in Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives. <br />

## Attrition by Years at Company:
Employees tend to leave within the first 2 years. <br />
## Gender-wise Attrition: 
Comparison between Male and Female employees. <br />


# Tools & Technologies Used
•	Microsoft Power BI – Dashboard design & visualization <br />
•	Excel / CSV Dataset – Data source for employee records <br />
•	Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation <br />
•	DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – For calculating attrition metrics and KPIs <br />


# Dashboard Features
•	Interactive filters for Department and Job Role <br />
•	Dynamic visuals for attrition breakdown by education, salary, and experience <br />
•	Easy-to-read charts showing workforce insights <br />
•	Attractive color-coded visuals for clear data storytelling <br />


# Key Learnings
•	Learned to calculate and visualize attrition rate using DAX measures. <br />
•	Enhanced understanding of HR data analytics and workforce management. <br />
•	Gained experience in Power BI data modeling and creating interactive dashboards. <br />


# Conclusion
The HR Analytics Dashboard provides actionable insights into employee attrition and demographics, helping HR professionals make strategic decisions to improve employee retention, satisfaction, and overall organizational performance.
